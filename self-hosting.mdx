---
title: "Plans & Billing"
description: "Understand AutoPilot's subscription plans and billing"
---

AutoPilot offers two plans: a free tier for getting started and a **Pro** subscription for users who need access to advanced features.

## Free tier

When you create an account you are on the free tier by default. The free tier lets you:

- Save credentials (API keys, webhook URLs, bot tokens) to your account
- View the workflow canvas and editor
- Explore the platform

Creating new workflows requires an active Pro subscription. If you try to create a workflow on the free tier, AutoPilot will prompt you to upgrade.

## Pro plan

The Pro plan unlocks full workflow creation and execution:

- **Create workflows** — Build new automation workflows on the visual canvas
- **Execute workflows** — Run workflows that use AI model nodes and notification nodes
- **All node types** — Use the complete set of triggers (Manual, HTTP Request, Google Form, Stripe) and action nodes without restriction

<Note>
  Billing is handled securely by [Polar](https://polar.sh). AutoPilot never stores your payment details — all payment processing happens on Polar's infrastructure.
</Note>

## How to upgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the upgrade prompt">
    You can start the upgrade flow in two ways:

    - When you try to use a Pro-only feature (such as saving a credential), AutoPilot shows an **Upgrade to Pro** dialog automatically.
    - Alternatively, go to your **Account Settings** and click **Upgrade**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete checkout via Polar">
    Click **Upgrade** in the dialog. You are redirected to a Polar-hosted checkout page. Enter your payment details and confirm the subscription.

    After a successful payment, Polar notifies AutoPilot and your account is upgraded immediately. You are redirected back to the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Pro features">
    Once your subscription is active, all credential types and node executions are unlocked. Any previously blocked actions will now work without the upgrade prompt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your subscription

To view, change, or cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Account Settings**.
2. Click **Manage Subscription**. You are redirected to the Polar customer portal.
3. In the portal you can update your payment method, download invoices, and cancel your subscription at any time.

<Warning>
  Cancelling your subscription downgrades your account to the free tier at the end of the current billing period. Workflow definitions and canvas data are preserved, but credential-dependent executions will stop working until you resubscribe.
</Warning>
